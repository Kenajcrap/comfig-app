Choose your preset

Maximum quality

Absolute maximum quality without caring about performance.
High quality

Enables graphical features missing from mid quality without making them very high quality.
Mid quality

Disables unoptimized features and optimizes the game without making the game look bad.
Competitive quality

The maximum performance you can get while enabling features that may give you an information advantage and disabling optimizations that may reduce accuracy.
Competitive performance

The maximum performance you can get without making the game too hard to play because of awful visual quality and glitches.
Maximum performance

Maximum performance without caring much about visibility or possible bugs.
Stripped performance

Negatively affects playability by a lot and disables very essential features in desperation for performance.
