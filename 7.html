mastercomfig 7




06.07.2018

mastercomfig 7 is a reimagining of mastercomfig and TF2 configs altogether. It comes with advanced customization and automation options to make the mastercomfig experience easier than ever.

Get ready for June 7th, when mastercomfig 7 will be released as a public beta.

In order to test extensive configuration changes in preparation for their release in mastercomfig 7, mastercomfig 6.8.0b1 is now available.

Feedback on this prerelease would be much appreciated to ensure mastercomfig 7's release is as smooth as possible.
